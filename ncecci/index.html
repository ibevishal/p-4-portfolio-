ABOUT NCECCI

The National Conference on Electronics, Communication, and Computational Intelligence (NCECCI) is a two-day event sponsored by the Bihar Council on Science and Technology (BCST) and organized by the Department of ECE, SPNREC, under the Department of Science, Technology, and Technical Education (DSTTE). This conference aims to bring together students, researchers, faculty, scientists, and industry professionals to explore the latest advancements in electronics, communication, and computational technologies. Expert speakers from top institutes like IITs, NITs, IIITs, and government organizations will share their knowledge on emerging trends such as semiconductor materials, metamaterials, 2D materials, communication systems, energy harvesting, and artificial intelligence. Attendees will gain valuable insights and opportunities to learn from leading experts in these cutting-edge fields.
